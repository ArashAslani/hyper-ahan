# Conversion Strategy

**Project:** HyperAhan  
**Phase:** 2 — Business & UX Blueprint  
**Objective:** Increase conversion from visitor to customer.

Conversion in steel commerce includes purchase requests, expert calls, price inquiries, WhatsApp leads, and checkout submissions.

---

## Conversion Principles

- Every page must have a clear next step.
- Expert consultation is a conversion path, not a fallback.
- Users must understand what happens after submitting a request.
- Mobile CTAs must be reachable and specific.
- Trust must appear before high-commitment actions.
- Content must support sales.

---

## Trust Signals

Trust signals should be specific and tied to the buying process.

Priority signals:

- visible phone number
- expert consultation
- clear price unit
- price-lock explanation
- known factories/brands
- transparent checkout agreement
- order status tracking
- business contact information
- after-submit expert follow-up
- delivery/shipping updates when available

Avoid unsupported claims such as “best price” unless provable.

---

## CTA Hierarchy

Primary CTAs:

- View products and prices
- Add to cart
- Submit purchase request

Secondary CTAs:

- Call expert
- WhatsApp expert
- Request price
- Use calculator

Tertiary CTAs:

- Read buying guide
- View related products
- Continue shopping
- Track order

Rules:

- Do not show multiple equal primary CTAs.
- Use direct outcome language.
- Pair self-service and expert CTAs on product pages.

---

## Phone Consultation

Phone is a primary conversion channel.

Use phone CTA on:

- homepage
- category pages
- product pages
- cart
- checkout support
- article consultation blocks
- no-result states

Phone CTA copy should position the call as expert help, not emergency support.

---

## WhatsApp

WhatsApp can reduce friction for users who prefer messaging.

Use only when the business can respond consistently.

Potential uses:

- send product inquiry
- send calculator result
- ask expert
- after-hours request

Rules:

- Do not replace structured checkout with untracked messaging.
- Use WhatsApp as lead capture and support.
- Avoid exposing unsupported promises about response time.

---

## Price Request

Price request captures users when:

- product is unavailable
- search has no result
- price is missing/stale
- user needs bulk quantity
- office is closed

Required fields should be minimal:

- product/category need
- phone number
- optional quantity/spec

Do not ask for full checkout details until user is ready.

---

## Calculator

Calculators increase confidence and lead quality.

Conversion paths:

- calculator result -> related products
- calculator result -> call expert
- calculator result -> price request
- calculator result -> cart in future

Rules:

- Explain estimates clearly.
- Do not present calculator output as final invoice.
- Use calculators for discovery, not just utility.

---

## Stock / Availability Indicators

Availability must be honest.

Possible states:

- available
- limited / call to confirm
- unavailable
- price on request
- unknown / expert confirmation required

Rules:

- Do not fake stock.
- If availability is not reliable, use “Call to confirm” instead of pretending certainty.
- Availability should influence CTA state.

---

## Urgency

Steel prices change quickly, but urgency must be truthful.

Acceptable urgency:

- cart price locked for 30 minutes
- price updated today
- market prices fluctuate
- submit request for expert confirmation

Avoid:

- fake countdowns
- fake scarcity
- invented “only X left” claims

---

## Social Proof

Future social proof can include:

- verified customer feedback
- completed order counts
- known factory/brand relationships
- response-time metrics
- case studies

Rules:

- Do not fabricate reviews.
- Do not display meaningless vanity metrics.
- Social proof must reinforce trust and conversion.

---

## Related Articles

Related articles should support decision-making.

Use on:

- category pages
- product pages
- calculator pages
- search no-result states

Purpose:

- explain product choice
- answer objections
- improve SEO internal linking

Each article must lead back to commercial actions.

---

## Related Products

Related products should help comparison.

Use on:

- product detail
- cart
- article
- category
- no-result state

Rules:

- Keep suggestions relevant.
- Explain why products are related.
- Do not distract from checkout once user is deep in cart/checkout.

---

## Conversion Measurement

Future metrics:

- homepage CTA click rate
- category-to-product rate
- product-to-call rate
- product-to-cart rate
- cart-to-checkout rate
- checkout completion rate
- article-to-product rate
- calculator-to-lead rate
- no-result-to-contact rate

Measurement must answer business questions, not collect data for its own sake.

## Page-Level Conversion Priorities

| Page | Primary Conversion | Secondary Conversion |
|------|--------------------|----------------------|
| Homepage | product/category/search click | call expert |
| Category | product click / call | related guide |
| Product | add to cart | call / WhatsApp |
| Blog article | related product/category | call / calculator |
| Calculator | related product/contact | article/category |
| Cart | checkout start | call expert |
| Checkout | submitted order | support/contact |
| Search | product click | price request |

---

## Conversion Anti-Patterns

Avoid:

- forcing account creation before product/cart
- hiding price behind contact when price is available
- long forms before intent is proven
- vague CTAs
- generic blog endings without product links
- checkout language that implies final payment when expert confirmation is required
- cluttered mobile screens with too many actions
