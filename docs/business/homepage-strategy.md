# Homepage Strategy

**Project:** HyperAhan  
**Phase:** 2 — Business & UX Blueprint  
**Purpose:** Define homepage sections before implementation.

The homepage must quickly answer: “Can I find today’s steel price and take the next step?”

---

## Homepage Role

The homepage is not a generic brand page. It is a conversion hub for:

- product discovery
- price discovery
- expert contact
- trust building
- SEO entry
- cart/checkout continuation

---

## Section 1 — Hero / Primary Entry

**Purpose:** Establish value and route users to price/product discovery.

**Business goal:** Convert first-screen visitors into product/category/search action.

**SEO goal:** Reinforce broad steel marketplace relevance.

**Conversion goal:** Click to products, search, category, or call.

**Expected user action:** Search product, view prices, open category, or call expert.

**Content guidance**

- concise headline about current steel prices and expert-assisted purchase
- primary CTA: View products/prices
- secondary CTA: Call expert
- optional trust cue: price lock / expert follow-up

---

## Section 2 — Search

**Purpose:** Help users with known intent skip navigation.

**Business goal:** Reduce time to product.

**SEO goal:** Not directly SEO, but supports engagement after landing.

**Conversion goal:** Product result click.

**Expected user action:** Search by product, size, factory, brand.

**Guidance**

- Search should be prominent on mobile.
- Placeholder examples should use real buyer language.
- No-result should route to price request/contact.

---

## Section 3 — Main Categories

**Purpose:** Give non-search users a clear product map.

**Business goal:** Route broad intent to category pages.

**SEO goal:** Strengthen internal links to major category pages.

**Conversion goal:** Category click.

**Expected user action:** Choose Rebar, Beam, Sheet, Profile, Pipe, Angle, Channel, Wire Rod, or Accessories.

**Guidance**

- Use simple category labels.
- Avoid technical subcategory overload on homepage.
- Each category should eventually link to an SEO category page.

---

## Section 4 — Daily Price Snapshot

**Purpose:** Show that HyperAhan is price-current and useful immediately.

**Business goal:** Build confidence and trigger product exploration.

**SEO goal:** Support broad “daily steel price” relevance.

**Conversion goal:** Click product/category from price list.

**Expected user action:** View product detail or full price list.

**Guidance**

- Show only a compact high-value subset on mobile.
- Include product, size, factory, unit, and price.
- Link to full category/product list.
- If prices are mock or stale, do not present as live production truth.

---

## Section 5 — Expert Consultation

**Purpose:** Turn uncertainty into contact.

**Business goal:** Capture users who need human confirmation.

**SEO goal:** Support trust and service intent.

**Conversion goal:** Phone/WhatsApp/consultation request.

**Expected user action:** Call expert or submit request.

**Guidance**

- Position consultation as a benefit.
- Explain that steel purchase is expert-confirmed.
- Keep the CTA short and direct.

---

## Section 6 — How Purchase Works

**Purpose:** Explain the expert-assisted order flow.

**Business goal:** Reduce checkout confusion and abandonment.

**SEO goal:** Support long-tail “how to buy steel online” intent.

**Conversion goal:** Increase confidence to add to cart/submit request.

**Expected user action:** Continue product discovery with clear expectations.

**Recommended steps**

1. Choose product.
2. Add to cart.
3. Submit request.
4. Expert confirms price/order.
5. Delivery/order tracking.

---

## Section 7 — Calculators / Buying Tools

**Purpose:** Help users estimate before buying.

**Business goal:** Convert uncertain users into qualified leads.

**SEO goal:** Capture calculator/weight-intent queries in future.

**Conversion goal:** Calculator use -> related product/contact.

**Expected user action:** Open relevant calculator.

**Guidance**

- Keep tools contextual.
- Output must link back to products/contact.
- Clarify estimates are not final invoices.

---

## Section 8 — Trust Signals

**Purpose:** Reduce perceived risk.

**Business goal:** Increase willingness to submit request or call.

**SEO goal:** Support E-E-A-T style trust signals.

**Conversion goal:** Continue to product/cart/contact.

**Potential trust signals**

- expert follow-up
- clear price units
- order tracking
- fast pro-forma invoice in future
- business contact details
- known factories/brands
- transparent agreement

Avoid unsupported claims.

---

## Section 9 — Popular / Trending Products

**Purpose:** Help users who do not know where to start.

**Business goal:** Increase product-detail visits.

**SEO goal:** Improve internal linking to commercial pages.

**Conversion goal:** Product click/add to cart.

**Expected user action:** Open product or category.

**Guidance**

- Base on real analytics later.
- Until then, use business-priority categories cautiously.
- Do not fake popularity claims.

---

## Section 10 — Blog / Buying Guides Preview

**Purpose:** Route informational users into educational content.

**Business goal:** Move uncertain buyers toward product selection.

**SEO goal:** Surface content clusters and internal links.

**Conversion goal:** Article read -> related products/contact.

**Expected user action:** Read guide or open related category.

**Guidance**

- Show buying guides, not generic news only.
- Each displayed article should have a commercial bridge.

---

## Section 11 — Support / Contact / After-Hours Request

**Purpose:** Capture users who are ready but blocked by timing or uncertainty.

**Business goal:** Prevent lost leads.

**SEO goal:** Reinforce business legitimacy.

**Conversion goal:** Phone, WhatsApp, or request form.

**Expected user action:** Contact or submit request.

**Guidance**

- Show working hours when available.
- Provide after-hours lead capture in future.

---

## Homepage Section Priority

Highest priority for first implementation:

1. Hero/search/product CTA
2. Categories
3. Daily price snapshot
4. Expert consultation
5. How purchase works
6. Trust signals
7. Blog/tools previews

Do not overload the homepage with every business idea at once.
